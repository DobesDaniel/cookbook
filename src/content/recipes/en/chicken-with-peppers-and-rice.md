---
title: "Baked Chicken with Peppers and Rice"
description: "Juicy chicken pieces baked with peppers, onion, garlic, and a touch of honey, served with rice."
language: "en"
translationKey: "chicken-with-peppers-and-rice"

preparationTime:
  min: 60
  max: 75

servings: 4

tags:
  - "Oven-baked"
  - "Healthy"
  - "Easy"

image: "/images/chicken_with_peppers_and_rice.webp"

ingredients:
  - "250 g rice"
  - "500-600 g chicken"
  - "4-5 fresh peppers"
  - "2 onions"
  - "2 cloves of garlic"
  - "2 tablespoons oil"
  - "1 tablespoon honey"
  - "Water"
  - "Salt and pepper"
---

1. Cut the chicken, peppers, and onions into large pieces.
2. Finely chop the garlic.
3. Place everything in a baking dish, then add the oil, honey, and seasoning to taste.
4. Add a little water for extra juices and mix everything together.
5. Cover the dish and bake for 45-60 minutes, until the chicken and vegetables are tender.
6. Cook the rice separately and serve it with the chicken.

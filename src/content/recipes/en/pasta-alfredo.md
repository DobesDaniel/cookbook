---
title: "Pasta Alfredo"
description: "Pasta in a creamy cauliflower and cheese sauce, served with chicken."
language: "en"
translationKey: "pasta-alfredo"

preparationTime:
  min: 30

servings: 4

tags:
  - "Pasta"
  - "Healthy"

image: "/images/pasta_alfredo.webp"

ingredients:
  - "1 cauliflower"
  - "50-100 g Parmesan or a similar cheese"
  - "1-2 cloves of garlic"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
  - "500 g chicken"
  - "Pasta"
  - "Salt"
  - "Pepper"
---

1. Cook the cauliflower until very soft.
2. Blend the cauliflower with the garlic and cheese until smooth.
3. Cook the pasta and mix it with the sauce.
4. Cut the chicken into portions, season with salt and pepper, and fry it in a pan until cooked through.
5. Add the chicken to the pasta.
6. Sprinkle with grated cheese to taste.
7. Serve and enjoy.

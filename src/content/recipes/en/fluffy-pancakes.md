---
title: "Fluffy pancakes"
description: "Simple breakfast pancakes served with yogurt and fruit."
language: "en"
translationKey: "fluffy-pancakes"

preparationTime:
  min: 25

servings: 4

tags:
  - "Breakfast"
  - "Sweet"

image: "/images/livance.png"

ingredients:
  - "250 g plain flour"
  - "2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "2 tablespoons sugar"
  - "A pinch of salt"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "300 ml milk"
  - "2 tablespoons melted butter"
---

1. Combine the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
2. Add the eggs, milk, and melted butter.
3. Mix briefly. A few small lumps are fine.
4. Cook the pancakes in a lightly greased pan until golden on both sides.
5. Serve with yogurt, fruit, or maple syrup.
